Near-max scope = dose-limit entries with Volume ≤ 0.1 cc. CV scope = complementary-volume (cold-volume) entries for parallel organs (Liver, Kidney_Cortex, Lungs), interpreted as "minimum volume spared from ≥ dose." Filters: pre-converted EQD2 or BED, α/β = 2.5, and re-irradiation intent excluded. Reference hierarchy: (1) highest Gy among records with QUANTEC tag and Fractions = Conventional for near-max; (2) conventional-fractionation CV value for parallel organs; (3) conventional fallback for OARs without a conventional-QUANTEC record (Biliary_Tree 80 Gy #768, BrachialPlexs 75 Gy #159, Bowel_Large 70 Gy #2008, Aorta 74 Gy #2673, Anal_Canal 51 Gy #2671).
